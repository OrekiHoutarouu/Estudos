Terraria

Terraria is a 2D, multiplayer, multiplataform and sandbox game that involves building, crafting, surviving, fighting, exploring, and a lot more! It was created in 2011 by the game developing company Re-Logic.

Terraria is known for its large amount of items, creatures, bosses and biomes. Compared to Minecraft, Terraria wins in the items and creatures variety regard.

Terraria is also known for its mods, one of the most famous is the "Calamity Mod", which adds dozens of new NPC's, events, creatures, bosses, biomes, leveling mechanics, harder difficult modes, songs and crafting recipes, that's a lot, right?
