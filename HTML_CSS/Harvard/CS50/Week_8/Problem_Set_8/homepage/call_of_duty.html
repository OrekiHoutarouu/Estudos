Call of Duty: Black Ops II

Call of Duty: Black Ops II is a 3D, multiplayer, multiplataform FPS (First Person Shooting) game! It was created in 2012 by the game developing company Treyarch and published by Activision.

Call of Duty: Black Ops II is one of the most famous game of the Call of Duty series. A First Person Shooting game that represents the war, which can be played with friends or in public servers.

Call of Duty: Black Ops II is famous because of its iconic game mode: Zombies. In this game mode you need to fight against zombies in lots of different maps in order to stay alive! You can also play this game mode with friends or alone, which can be pretty scary for some people!
