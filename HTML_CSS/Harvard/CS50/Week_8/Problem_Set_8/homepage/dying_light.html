Dying Light

Dying Light is a 3D, open world, multiplayer, multiplataform survival horror zombie game! It was created in 2015 by the game developing company Techland and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment.

Dying Light is one of the best zombie games. In the game campaign, the main character, Crane, is a "government spy", he works for a company called GRE. Crane is sent to a city called Harran, an apocaliptic city full of zombies, to find the cure of this kind of disease. So Crane works hard, kill zombies, fight gangsters, learns parkour, falls in love with a girl and a lot more! It's a very graphical game, with a lot of blood and violency.

Dying light has a day and night system. During the day the zombies are slow and weak, but in the night they become faster, stronger and other kinds of zombies starts to appear, the "Volatiles", these are the strongest zombies in the game.

Dying Light can also be played on multiplayer mode, with a max of 4 friends, so you can fight zombies together!
