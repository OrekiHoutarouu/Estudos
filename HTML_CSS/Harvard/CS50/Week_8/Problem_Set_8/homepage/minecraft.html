Minecraft

Minecraft is one of the best games in history and it's also the most sold game in the moment that i'm writing this, with over 300.000.000 sales.

Minecraft is a 3D, multiplayer, multiplataform and sandbox game that involves building, crafting, surviving, fighting, exploring, and a lot more! It was created in 2009 by the swedish developer Markus Persson, also known as "Notch". Later, Minecraft got developed and published by Mojang Studios.

Minecraft has one of the biggest and most creative communities in the gaming area. Every year the Minecraft community get together fot an event, the Mob Vote. The Mob Vote is basically a voting for a new mob (creature) for the game, which can be a terrible monster or a cute turtle, who knows?
