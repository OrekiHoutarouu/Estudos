Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free, 2D, multiplayer, multiplataform ranked fighting game! It was created in 2014 by the game developing company Blue Mammoth Games.

Brawlhalla is a ranked game, and the ranks are: Tin, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum and Diamond. In every season of the game, you have to fight to other players using combos, swords, bows, axes and a lot more in order to rank up. When the season ends, you gain points, the points are called "Glory", and you can use your Glory to buy items inside the game, like skins and pets.

But if you don't want to play ranked games, you can play with your friends, there are lots of fun game modes, including soccer... using swords! You can play with a max of 8 friends.

Besides all of that information, Brawlhalla also has a large E-Sports community, with competitions all over the world! Also, lots of Brawlhalla characters are actually crossovers with other games!
